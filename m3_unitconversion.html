Unit Conversions
Temperature




To convert Celsius to Fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing 'C' with your temperature in celsius: (c x 9/5) + 32
Weight




To convert Kilograms to Pounds yourself, use this formula relacing the 'kg' with your weight in Kilograms: kg x 2.205
Distance




To convert Kilometers to Miles yourself, use this formula replacing the 'km' with your distance in kilometers: km/1.609
